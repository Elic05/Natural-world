How it work

τὰ φυσικὰ is a Generative Art NTF collection of trees inspired to fractals, each art is unique and unpredictable . The collection is developed in Processing thought the implementation random variables composing the elemenths of each art piece.

Each NFT is composed of 4 different elemenths: tree branches - leaves colours - watercolour hand painted background - seed

The first step 100 trees will be created and sold accompanied by a little seed which will generate a new tree in 30 days

All holders will together build a growing community around τὰ φυσικὰ. As a reward and proof of membership to each member will be given the project token as a benefit.
