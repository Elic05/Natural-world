02 : The Project

τὰ φυσικὰ is a generative art NFT collection inspired to fractals, generated by the union of painted images and coding. Blockchain and code have a strong connection with the outside world and art.

Trees as natural fractals are the protagonists of τὰ φυσικὰ and common artistic language between us and nature. Each art is unique and unpredictable, generated by the union of 4 components: tree branches, leaves colours, hand painted background, a seed.
